You can close,logoff,standby,restart or shutdown the computer after you complete the conversion automatically using this setting.

Details:

Do Nothing->It does nothing after you complete the conversion.

Close Converter->It closes your converter after you complete the conversion.

Log Off->It logs off your computer after you complete the conversion.

Stand By->It stand by your computer after you complete the conversion.

Restart->It restarts your computer after you complete the conversion.

Shutdown->It shutdowns your computer after you complete the conversion.

Note:
These settings will be saved when you make the changes.Be sure of these settings before you start conversion.Otherwise your computer may be restarted or shutdown after the complition of conversion and you may lose your working data.
